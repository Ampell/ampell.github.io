Venue - Qudos Bank Arena, Sydney

Qudos Bank Arena, located in the heart of Sydney, is one of the city’s premier live event venues. Boasting a seating capacity of over 18,000, the arena is designed to offer an exceptional viewing experience with state-of-the-art acoustics, ergonomic seating, and unobstructed sightlines. Its modern facilities and thoughtfully arranged layout ensure that every guest enjoys both comfort and convenience, whether attending a concert, sporting event, or other live performances. The venue’s emphasis on quality and accessibility makes it a standout destination for unforgettable, immersive experiences.
