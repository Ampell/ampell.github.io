Accessibility

MUSICPIT is committed to creating an inclusive experience for every guest. The venue features wheelchair-accessible entrances, dedicated seating options, and assistive technologies to ensure that all music lovers can enjoy the show comfortably. Please contact our team beforehand so we can arrange suitable services for your needs! Email MUSICPIT@gmail.com for any inquiries.

Information

At MUSICPIT, all ticket purchases are non-refundable and non-transferable. Please verify your order details carefully before finalizing your purchase. Arrive at least 30 minutes early with a clear, scannable digital ticket or printed copy, along with a valid photo ID. Children under 12 are not permitted, and guests under 16 must be accompanied by an adult. Tickets must be legible and complete, as discrepancies could delay your entry.
