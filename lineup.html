Lineup - Musicpit

Prepare to witness three of the hottest names in hip-hop take the stage. From raw energy to hypnotic vibes, these artists will leave you breathless.

Playboi Carti

Playboi Carti is a pioneer of the modern trap sound, known for his wild energy, baby voice, and genre-pushing style. With iconic albums like *Die Lit* and *Whole Lotta Red*, he’s built a cult following and defined an era. His live shows are pure chaos—in the best way—transforming crowds into mosh pits. Carti is more than an artist; he’s a movement of sound, fashion, and pure presence.

Ken Carson

Ken Carson delivers explosive, futuristic rap with a signature rage-fueled sound. A rising force under Carti’s Opium label, he blends hard beats, melodic flow, and sleek visuals to make something entirely his own. His projects *Project X* and *X* showcase a sharp voice of the next wave. On stage, he’s electric—raw, loud, and impossible to ignore.

Destroy Lonely

Destroy Lonely brings a smooth, ambient twist to trap, combining dreamy production with sleek, fashion-forward flair. Known for *NO STYLIST*, his moody flows and distinctive voice make every track feel like a vibe. His live sets are atmospheric and entrancing—cool, collected, but heavy on impact. Lonely is defining a new era of stylish, introspective sound.
